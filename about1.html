About Us


CHAWAN TAILOR provides exceptional bespoke and made-to-measure services for modern women. From luxury bridalwear blouse, to beautifully-cut blouses, to distinctive

eveningwear blouses; create your dream designer blouse with Chawan Tailor for a look that compliments both your figure and personality. Ideal for special occasions but

sensational for day-to-day life. With traditional workmanship and an exacting fit process, expect a quality finish, designed to fully flatter your body. We offer an extensive variety

of luxury fabrics for you to choose from – but don’t worry! – our master advice is at-hand. Contact us today for a friendly and professional consultation. Just bring your

thoughts, your inspirations, and yourself.
